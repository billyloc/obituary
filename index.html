Joe Bird

03/16/1955 - 02/10/2021

We Love You & Miss You!

Previous Next

Joe Bird, 65, of Albuquerque New Mexico, passed away peacefully surrounded by loved ones on February 10th, 2021. He was born March 16th, 1955, in Jacksonville Florida to William and Lois Bird. Joe was a husband, father, and grandfather who will be greatly missed and lovingly remembered. He enjoyed relaxing afternoons with a glass of Canadian Mist and Coke while he watched old westerns. He loved listening to country music, trips to the mountains, cooking, and most of all spending time with his family. Joe is survived by, his wife Vicky of 34 years, his son Charlie (Carlos) his wife Stephanie, his Son Billy his wife Sasha, his grandchildren who knew him as "Paqua", Ciera, Carlos, Isabella, and Emiliano, and his brother Bill and sister Marie and her husband Steve. Joe will forever live on in our hearts and memories until we are reunited again, we love you Dad, Paqua, Joe. The family will be holding a virtual memorial service for Joe on Saturday February 20th, 2021 at 1:00 PM via zoom. Please contact family for virtual invitation.
